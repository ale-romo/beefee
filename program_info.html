Insurance Carrier

Mexico auto insurance is underwritten by the insurance company and provided by MacAfee and Edwards, Inc., a US Insurance Surplus Line broker.

Scope

Mexico auto insurance is underwritten by the insurance company and provided by MacAfee and Edwards, Inc., a US Insurance Surplus Line broker.

Policy Plans

  • Daily: This option allows you to obtain insurance for a trip into Mexico for up to 30 days.
  • Semi-annual: This option allows you to obtain auto insurance for a trip into Mexico for 180 days.
  • Annual: Provides you with auto insurance coverage while in Mexico for an entire year (365 days) at a very convenient price.

Packages

Economy

This package provides only damages that you may cause to others, plus legal defense and basic road assistance while driving in Mexico.
Important, no coverage is provided to your vehicle if this option is selected.

Standard

This package includes the Economy coverage plus physical damage and total theft to your vehicle while driving in Mexico.

Premium

The Premium Package includes the Economy and the Standard coverages, plus covers vandalism, partial theft, extension of liability, USA labor rate when your vheicle it’s involved in an accident and medical evaluation.

Risk Description

Economy

CSL Liability

Covers the authorized driver’s liability to third parties for property damage and bodily injury resulting from an accident as a CSL.

Medical Expenses

Expenses incurred by the Insured or an occupant of the Insured vehicle by reason of bodily injuries sustained while riding the cab and in the designed area for passengers caused by fire, collision or upset of the vehicle described herein.

Legal Aid

Covers the legal defense of the Insured or the Insured's authorized driver, when the involvement of the insured vehicle is in a traffic accident leads to criminal and/or administrative action.

Basic Road Assistance

Covers emergency roadside service, towing due to mechanical failure or breakdown, transportation of passengers to the nearest town where vehicle is towed.

Standard

Physical Damage

Covers vehicle damage or loss resulting from collision, upset and glass breakage (collision, overturn, window breakage, fire, lightning and/or explosion, strikes, labor disturbances, cyclones, hurricanes, tornados, hail storm, earthquake, volcanic eruptions, avalanches, flooding of rivers) -Compulsory deductible- Total Theft Covers total loss of the vehicle as well as a loss or damage incurred as a consequence of said total theft. -Compulsory deductible- Note: Partial Theft is not covered.

Physical Damage

Covers vehicle damage or loss resulting from collision, upset and glass breakage (collision, overturn, window breakage, fire, lightning and/or explosion, strikes, labor disturbances, cyclones, hurricanes, ornados, hail storm, earthquake, volcanic eruptions, avalanches, flooding of rivers) -Compulsory deductible-

Total Theft

Covers total loss of the vehicle as well as a loss or damage incurred as a consequence of said total theft. -Compulsory deductible- Note: Partial Theft is not covered.

Premium

Vandalism

Covers intentional or malicious acts by one or various persons that result in damage to the insured vehicle, without any other intention than destruction.

Partial Theft

Covers theft or original equipment installed inside the insured vehicle by the manufacturer.

Labor Rate

Covers the partial material damages at the insured place of residence.

Liability Extension

Covers damages caused by the policy holder when driving any other private vehicle similar to the insured vehicle.

Roadside, Medical And Traveling Assistance Coverage

Provides a broader and ample emergency roadside service, including various additional services as repatriation, tow services due to mechanical breakdown, medical evacuation and more.

Medical Evacuation

Covers the specialized medical transfer to the most adequate Hospital inside of Mexico due to an illness or injury.

Underwritten Notes

  • Private use vehicles only.
  • Economy package can be offer for vehicles up to 20 years.
  • Standard and Premium packages can only be offer to vehicles up to 15 years.
  • Driver(s) must have a valid drivers license and be at least 21 years of age.
  • No coverage is granted to full time residents of the Mexican Republic, whether citizens of Canada, US or Mexico.
  • Salvage title vehicles are not eligible for this program.
  • Auto insurance program is subject to the insurance company general conditions.
